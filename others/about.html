About Us

What is the Band of the Hawk

We, the band of the hawk, our mission is to address the fundamentals of video games and possiblities of future advancements on video games. Our journey started when we conducted research on topics of video games, such as evolution of video games, from arcades to platforming games, gaming engines that designs revolutionary and innoative games that celebrated and awarded today, gaming platforms that expand cross-platforming and provides players experiences on mulitple devices, VR/AR gaming that demostrates interactive fun and Esports that conducts tournaments that provides a larger community and connects to a bigger world. Some of us consists of university students, musicians, gamers, gym-goers and anime enjoyers but we grow to be a family more than a band or a group.


Inspiration from the team name comes from hit manga Berserk which envetually inspired many games including Software's Darks Souls and Elden Ring. The name "Band of the Hawk" is a fictional name for a mercenary band, so don't get confuse with a mercenary band with a group of university students.
