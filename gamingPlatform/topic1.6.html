Arcades

Recent trends & developments

Arcades have served for several decades and have gone trhough various trends changes over the years:

  • VR and AR games: Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) games have become increasingly popular in arcades. These games offer a more immersive gaming experience that allows players to interact with the game environment in semi-realistic worlds. Click here for more info
  • Classic games and nostalgia: There has been a renewed interest in classic arcade games, particularly among younger players who are seen interest in playing them. Remastered versions of classic games are implement through consoles or portable arcade machines allows younger and old audiences to enjoy nostalgia Click here for more info
  • Competitive Gaming: Esports has become increasingly popular in arcades where players compete in tournaments, usually fighting games, for exclusive rewards and recognition. And eventually, it will be a sport category in the Olympics. Click here for more info

"The modern arcade game experience is all about creating a social environment where people can come together, have fun and learn new skills. The latest trends include immersive virtual reality experiences, retro games that tap into a sense of nostalgia, and interactive games that blur the line between the physical and digital worlds. As we move forward, we can expect to see arcades continue to evolve and innovate, providing new and exciting ways for people to engage with gaming culture."

- Dr. Mia Consalvo, Canada Research Chair in Game Studies and Design.
